## Boolean Algebra

Boolean algebra is a form of mathematics that deals with statements and their Boolean values. <b>In computer science</b>, Boolean Algebra is utilized in the design and study of algorithms, particularly in fields that require decision-making processes. It's vital in database query optimization, where Boolean logic is utilized to filter and obtain specific data based on circumstances.

Accordingly subject here is the allowed Boolean Algebra operations:

  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Operation</th>

      <td>OR Operation</td>
      <td>| or ∨</td>

      <td>XOR Operation (Exclusive OR)</td>
      <td>^ or ⊕</td>

    <tr>
      <td>NOT Operation</td>
      <td>¬ or ∼</td>

    </tr>

    <tr>
      <td>Logical equivalence</td>
      <td>= or ↔</td>

      <td>> or ⇒</td>

      </td>
    </tr>
